Our Gym Journey

PowerFlex began in 2015 with a simple vision — to redefine fitness culture in India. From a single 2,000 sq ft training space in Mumbai, we grew into a multi-branch elite transformation center. We focus on science-based programming, discipline, and a results-driven community. Over the years, we have helped thousands of members lose weight, build muscle, compete professionally, and improve their overall lifestyle.
