Our Journey

From a single training studio to a national transformation brand.

Founded in 2015 in Mumbai, PowerFlex Gym began as a strength-focused training facility built on discipline, science, and performance. Over the years, we evolved into a full-scale transformation ecosystem offering structured programming, certified coaching, sports conditioning, and lifestyle management systems. Our growth came from one principle: measurable results. Thousands of members have successfully transformed their physique, confidence, and mental resilience through our system-driven approach. Today we operate multiple branches across India and continue expanding with the same elite standards.
