Our Journey

From a single training facility to a multi-branch elite performance center.

Founded in 2015, PowerFlex Gym began as a small strength training studio in Mumbai. With a mission to elevate fitness culture in India, we focused on scientific programming, disciplined coaching, and measurable transformation systems. Over the years, our commitment to excellence helped thousands of members lose weight, build muscle, improve athletic performance, and transform their lifestyle. Today, PowerFlex stands as a premium fitness brand with multiple branches, certified trainers, and a growing community of dedicated achievers.
